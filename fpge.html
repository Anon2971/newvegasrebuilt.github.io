Collection 10/10: Functional Post Game Ending

We're now onto The Big One. The most important piece of cut content mod. This is the last Collection in the guide as its content will activate once you complete The Battle of Hoover Dam. It can be safely installed as you approach the endgame.

Functional Post Game Ending (FPGE)

Installation instructions:

  • Main File - Functional Post Game Ending
Obsidian planned to implement dramatic changes to New Vegas depending on your choices in the climactic battle. Unfortunately they were replaced with slides as they didnâ€™t have enough time to implement these features to a high quality, but much of the code was left in the game files. Kazopert did a fantastic job restoring this in FPGE. It allows you to continue playing after finishing the main quest with changes to the world depending on what ending you went with.

Finishing Notes

Congratulations! You now have modded Fallout New Vegas pretty exhaustively. Click the Deploy button one last time, then click New Vegas in Vortex to start the game. Enjoy!
DO NOT bother the authors of any of the mods in the guide if you have issues. Google, the mod page, Nexus Mods Discord and Reddit are good resources for troublesooting. This guide I've written contains a ridiculous number of mods and any issues you're experiencing are more likely due to conflicts in some way with one of the mods I recommended rather than anything wrong with that specific mod. However, I've again written this guide gradually over years of playthroughs. So you shouldn't experience any problems outside of the ones I mentioned on the first page if you followed this guide properly.
